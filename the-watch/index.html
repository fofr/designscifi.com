What are you getting for Christmas?

23 Dec
Siri, what is my wife buying me for Christmas?
Rebecca is giving you a PS4 video game, a recipe book and an expensive watch
Is that enough detail?
Yes
Have I bought enough for her?
No
You should buy her something more romantic
❆
27 Dec
Siri, my wife didn’t give me the watch. Did she return it?
No
Are you sure it was for me?
Yes. It was delivered gift wrapped with the message, “To my true love xxx”
How much was it?
Over £1,000
What has she done with it?
I don’t know
What should I do?
I will ask her about the watch
